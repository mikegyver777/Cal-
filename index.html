How it works. Bars run both directions, inset from each edge and spaced on-center: bars = (grid ÷ spacing) + 1 each way. Runs longer than one stick are lapped (lapping factor × bar diameter per joint). Weight uses standard bar mass per foot; the waste allowance adds a percentage for extra laps and cutoffs. Since rebar sells in whole sticks, the count rounds up — so the actual weight and cost reflect full bars.
IDEA Construction Incorporated Estimates are for planning and material takeoff only. Always follow the engineer's structural drawings and specified lap lengths for final placement and ordering.
